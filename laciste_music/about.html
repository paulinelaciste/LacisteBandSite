About

Laciste is an electronic music duo from Los Angeles. Their sound combines electronic dance, ethereal vocals and RnB music, drawing from their soul, jazz, hip hop, classical, experimental, and rock background. Zane Guindon is originally from McKinney, TX and has lived in Los Angeles for 10 years. Zane does everything from songwriting, producing, recording and mixing. Singer Pauline Laciste studied classical piano and fell in love with electronic music composition as a student at NYU. The California native has written music ranging from classical chamber pieces to electronic sound collages and scores for short films.
